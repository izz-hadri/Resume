Project Summary (Group Project)

COVID-19 is a respiratory disease that can be spread via coughing, sneezing, and exhaling air by infected individuals. Even now, there are many people who neglect the SOP which causes the number of active cases of COVID-19 to increase. This game aims to bring peopleâ€™s attention towards the practice of SOP which should be part of our new norm.

Objective

  • To raise awareness of the new norm that needs to be adapted to fit with the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • To implement the right lifestyle through the game in the real world situation.
  • Search citizens that do not follow the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP).
  • If the player click on the wrong citizens, the time limit will be reduced.
